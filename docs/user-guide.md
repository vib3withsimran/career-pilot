# Career Pilot: New User Onboarding Guide

Welcome to **Career Pilot**! We're here to help you land your dream job at warp speed. This guide will walk you through setting up your profile, using our core features, and answering common questions to ensure you get the most out of the platform.

## 🚀 Getting Started Walkthrough

### 1. Create Your Account
Start by heading to the Sign Up page. You can register using your email and password, or use quick social logins like Google or LinkedIn.  
![Sign Up Page](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Sign+Up+Page)

### 2. Set Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We care about your security! Upon your first login, we highly recommend setting up 2FA.
- Go to **Security Settings**.
- Click **Enable 2FA** and scan the QR code with your chosen authenticator app.
- Make sure to **save your backup codes** in a secure location!

### 3. Complete Your Profile
A complete profile helps you stand out to recruiters and allows our AI to give you better recommendations. Head to **My Profile** and add your core skills, experience, and current job role.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I have to pay to use Career Pilot?**
A: Core features like resume uploading and basic community access are entirely free. Some advanced AI enhancement features may have usage limits depending on your tier.

**Q: How accurate is the ATS scoring?**
A: Our ATS analyzer uses the same industry-standard checks (keyword matching, formatting sanity checks, and verb strength) to mimic real applicant tracking systems. Achieving a >80% score significantly boosts your chances of passing an initial screen.

**Q: I lost my 2FA Authenticator app. How do I log in?**
A: You can log in using one of the Backup Codes you downloaded during the initial 2FA setup process. If you lose both, please reach out to our platform administrators.

**Q: Can I download my enhanced resume as a PDF?**
A: Yes! Once our AI enhances your resume's bullet points, you can download a beautifully formatted PDF version directly to your device.
